Our Story

At LAKBAY, we are revolutionizing public transportation in the Philippines by seamlessly integrating traditional jeepney services with modern technology. Drawing inspiration from the dynamic urban landscapes of cities like Manila, our platform addresses the everyday challenges faced by commuters.
