About Us

Revolutionizing Public Transportation in the Philippines

Our Story

At LAKBAY, we are revolutionizing public transportation in the Philippines by seamlessly integrating traditional jeepney services with modern technology. Drawing inspiration from the dynamic urban landscapes of cities like Manila, our platform addresses the everyday challenges faced by commuters. Unlike general navigation applications such as Waze, we specialize in jeepney tracking, enabling commuters to connect with drivers along shared routes for efficient, on-demand rides.

Our Mission

Our mission is to enhance urban mobility by making commuting safer, more efficient, and accessible to all. We are committed to empowering local jeepney drivers and commuters, fostering community connections while minimizing wait times and transportation expenses. Through our platform, we aim to elevate the jeepney experience into a reliable, technology-driven solution that benefits both passengers and operators.

How It Works

For Commuters: Monitor nearby jeepneys traveling your route, submit ride requests, and arrange pickups at convenient points along the way. Eliminate the need for fixed stops and enjoy flexible travel.

For Drivers: Utilize a dedicated interface to view ride requests from commuters, optimize your route, and maximize earnings by accommodating additional passengers. It functions as an intelligent assistant for your jeepney operations.

Powered by Technology: Leveraging GPS tracking and advanced matching algorithms, we facilitate secure, efficient pairings while upholding user privacy and data protection standards.

Our Values

  • Community-Oriented: We champion local jeepney drivers and advocate for sustainable, cost-effective transportation solutions.
  • Innovative Heritage: We harmonize the rich tradition of jeepney culture with state-of-the-art application features.
  • Prioritizing Safety: We implement stringent user verification processes and continuous monitoring to ensure a trustworthy environment.
  • Inclusive Access: Our platform is designed to serve a diverse range of users, from students to professionals, promoting equitable and streamlined commuting experiences.
